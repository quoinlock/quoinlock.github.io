Use Cases

Discover how Quoinlock helps different roles in the publishing industry

For Publishers

Catalog Management

As a publisher managing a handful or hundreds of titles, keeping track of book listings across every retail platform is nearly impossible. Quoinlock monitors your entire catalog automatically, alerting you to pricing errors, metadata inconsistencies, or availability issues before they impact sales.

Key Benefits

  • Monitor unlimited ISBNs across all platforms
  • Catch unauthorized price changes immediately
  • Ensure metadata consistency everywhere
  • Protect your brand and revenue
For Authors

Book Launch Monitoring

You've just published your book, and you want to make sure it appears correctly on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, and other retailers. Instead of manually checking each site daily, Quoinlock watches your listings and notifies you the moment your book goes live or if anything changes.

Key Benefits

  • Know instantly when your book goes live
  • Verify cover images and descriptions are correct
  • Track pricing across all retailers
  • Focus on writing, not manual checking
For Retailers

Competitive Price Monitoring

As an online bookstore, you need to stay competitive. Quoinlock helps you monitor competitor pricing across the market, ensuring your prices remain attractive while maintaining healthy margins. Get alerts when competitors change prices or when opportunities arise.

Key Benefits

  • Track competitor pricing in real-time
  • Identify pricing opportunities
  • Maintain competitive advantage
  • Automate market research
For Bookstores

Local Inventory Verification

Your independent bookstore has an online presence on multiple platforms like Bookshop.org and your own website. Quoinlock ensures consistency, pricing, and book information synchronization across online listings.

Key Benefits

  • Verify listings across multiple platforms
  • Maintain accurate pricing everywhere
  • Save time on manual compeditor checks
For Self-Publishers

Multi-Platform Publishing

You publish on KDP, IngramSpark, Draft2Digital, and other platforms. Each distributor pushes your book to different retailers. Quoinlock helps you track where your book appears, ensuring all platforms show the correct information and that your promotional pricing updates propagate correctly.

Key Benefits

  • Track distribution across all channels
  • Verify promotional pricing takes effect
  • Monitor metadata across platforms
  • Maximize your publishing reach
For Librarians

Cataloging & MARC Management

Managing library catalog records requires precision and efficiency. Quoinlock provides powerful tools to monitor Library of Congress Catalog records, view and compare MARC21 data, generate Cutter numbers, and translate technical MARC records into human-readable formats for cataloging workflows.

Key Benefits

  • Monitor Library of Congress Catalog records
  • Full MARC21 viewer with detailed field analysis
  • ISBN MARC Comparison View for record validation
  • Cutter Creator Tool for call number generation
  • Human Readable MARC records for easy review
